Usage Guidelines
Normal lockup — use on white or very light backgrounds. The icon’s white details blend with white pages.
Inverse lockup — use on dark backgrounds matching #1a2332.
Horizontal lockup — primary format for headers, navigation, and wide contexts.
Vertical lockup — use for square contexts, social profiles, splash screens, and stacked layouts.
Platform avatars — use the square PNG icon files (512px or 1024px).
Minimum size — icon should not appear smaller than 32px in height.
Clear space — maintain padding equal to at least 50% of the icon height around the lockup.
